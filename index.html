Haynes Heaton MD

I am a computational biologist, computer scientist, and medical doctor. I'm interested in computational and algorithmic methods to improve basic science and healthcare.

Currently I am a PhD student at the Sanger Institute and Cambridge University working on various algorithmic and data analysis problems in genomics and single cell transcriptomics. Previously I was working at 10X Genomics working on fixing the limitations of current nextgen short read sequencing, gaining long range genetic information through molecular barcodes. My projects focused on mapping into repeat regions of the genome as well as phasing and various uses of phasing information.
